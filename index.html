Tinting corrects colours in brickwork, stone and concrete

By creating bespoke tints and applying them with a keen eye, we are able to disguise ugly repairs, unite extensions with the original building or perhaps create a whole new appearance throughout. This process provides a very natural, permanent colour change and comes with a 25 year guarantee.

With considerable background in building repair and conservation, we always begin with a considered assessment of the situation as unsightly masonry may be affected by more than simply colour differences. Once you have seen some examples of our work, please get in touch and send us some photos.
